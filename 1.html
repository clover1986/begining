Study

I, Aiden Juntaek Oh, graduated from the Aviation Technician - Aircraft Maintenance diploma program at Centennial College with accreditation in May, 2015. I have a strong knowledge of and good skills in repairing a wide range of electrical and mechanical systems - engines, flight, hydraulic, undercarriage and propulsion systems. Through my education, I have gained knowledge of specialized methods and techniques of aircraft maintenance and the interaction of aircraft systems to assist in evaluating and classifying flight defects. I also possess experience working on Cessna 100 series, Piper Seminole, Piper Aztec, Beech 18 aircraft as well as Bell 47 and 206 helicopters.
