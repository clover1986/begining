Career

During my time in the Republic of Korea Air Force, my responsibilities included inspection, repair and maintenance of various aircraft machinery, components and equipment. I often worked in groups, and learned to work together with members of my team to complete tasks on a deadline. My military experience also taught me discipline, and I have an excellent work ethic as a result.

While working as an Aircraft Component Technician at Aeropol Aviation Services, I have achieved assembly, disassembly, and inspection skills, as well as test equipment experience. Since I carry out maintenance tasks with many different aircraft components in accordance with Component Maintenance Manual (CMM), Service Bulletins (SB) and Airworthiness Directives (AD), I can easily follow and interpret technical drawings and instructions.

Currently, I am working as an Aircraft Engine Assembler at Pratt & Whitney Canada.
